✨ About Us

Iridis AI is an AI lab founded by two passionate high school students. We're dedicated to building AI that is not only powerful but also affordable, accessible, and fun for everyone. Our mission is to democratize AI technology and make it available across different languages and cultures.

🚀 What We Do

Multilingual LLMs

We fine-tune diverse language conversational LLMs that understand and respond effectively in various cultural contexts, with initial focus on Japanese, Chinese (Mandarin & Cantonese), English, and Korean.

Text-to-Speech

We fine-tune TTS systems that deliver optimal performance and cultural relevance, particularly for Japanese, Chinese (Cantonese) and English.

Coming Soon

We're expanding into developing LLMs from scratch and creating benchmarks for language models to push the boundaries of accessible AI technology.
